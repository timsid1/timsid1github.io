PAKISTAN SNACKS OR STREET FOOD

Pakistani Snacks or Street Food, comprise food items in Pakistan that are quick to prepare, spicy, usually fried, and eaten in the evening or morning with tea or with any one of the meals as a side dish. A given snack may be part of a local culture, and its preparation and popularity can vary from place to place.
These snacks are often prepared and sold by hawkers on footpaths, railway stations and other such places, although they may also be served at restaurants.
